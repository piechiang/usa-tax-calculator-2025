## Overview

South Dakota has **NO state income tax** and has never had one. Known for its **trust industry** and business-friendly environment with no corporate income tax or personal property tax.

## Key Features

- **No state income tax** (0% rate)
- **No corporate income tax**
- **No personal property tax**
- **No business inventory tax**
- **Strong trust industry** (dynasty trusts, financial privacy)
- Revenue from sales tax (4.2% state), tourism, bank franchise tax

## Trust Industry

South Dakota has a thriving $500+ billion trust industry due to:
- No state income tax on trust income
- **Dynasty trusts** (perpetual trusts allowed)
- Strong privacy and asset protection laws
- Flexible trust statutes
- Attracts wealthy families and financial institutions
